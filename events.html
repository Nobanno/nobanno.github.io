International Mother Language Day 2023

The 21st of February is known internationally as International Mother Language Day, chosen because of the tragic — yet catalyzing— events that transpired on that day in 1952, in Bangladesh. We celebrate International Mother Language Day, a day to acknowledge the importance of linguistic and cultural diversity. Let's take a moment to recognize and appreciate the value of our mother languages, which hold our cultural heritage and memories. On that context, Nobanno proudly organized a cultural program to celebrate International Mother Language Day. This event aspired to be a mosaic of performances, music, poetry, art, and cuisine. Let's also work towards preserving endangered languages and promoting multilingualism.

Farewell to Graduates - 2023

We celebrated the end of the Spring semester with a summer BBQ to say farewell to our dear recent graduates. As some of them moved to new places, it was the perfect opportunity to show our love and appreciation for them. For many of us, this was the last chance to spend time with them for a while, and we wanted to make it a memorable one. Congratulations, Graduate Terps! Good luck on your new beginnings!

Fresher's Reception 2023

"Nobanno" felt excited to announce its upcoming 2nd Annual Picnic and Freshers' Reception, scheduled for September 9, 2023 (Saturday). This event promised an excellent opportunity to reconnect with familiar faces from the past, while also extending a warm welcome to the new incoming students. We crafted our events to include brief introductory sessions for newcomers, gift exchanges, engaging games, and delightful lunches. Lastly, we feel honored and grateful to have Mayor Dr. Fazlul Kabir, The City of College Park, MD in our event and it meant a great deal to all of us.

Flood Fundraiser: A Colloboration with Ankur International

As you may have heard, recent devastating floods have wreaked havoc on the lives of countless individuals and families in Bandarban, Bangladesh. In times like these, every bit of support makes a significant difference. One of the agenda of “Nobanno” is to fundraise/donate for good causes once a year. Just like previous year, 'Nobanno' managed to collect donations to help the flood-affected area of Bandarban. The collected donations were effectively channeled through Ankur International, ensuring that the assistance reached those in need with the greatest impact. Big thanks to everyone who contributed and supported this noble initiative. Photo Credit: Dhaka Tribune
