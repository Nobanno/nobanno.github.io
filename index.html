Nobanno-Bangladesh Graduate Students' Association

"Nobanno" is the association for students coming from Bangladesh to University of Maryland at College Park. This association aims to provide a platform to promote and nurture the culture of Bangladesh on the campus of the University of Maryland, College Park. This association is a non-religious, non-political association, functioning as a cultural organization.

Flood Fundraiser: A Colloboration with Ankur International

We hope you are already aware of the dire situation of flooding in the Sylhet region, Bangladesh. It is already reported that this flood has already resulted in at least 25 casualties and is turning out to be a grave threat to the livelihood to the residents. With forecasts indicating that the situation will worsen in the coming days, we think that this is high time for us to act for the people impacted by the flooding. Given that one of the agenda of “Nobanno” is to fundraise/donate for good cause once a year, we are collecting donations from the community members to help the flood affected people. We managed to raise 645 USD in total for the flood devastated residents.
